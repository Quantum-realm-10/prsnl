My Beloved Zawjaa — My Love

You are my tranquility, my blessing, and my forever love.

NA
أَنْتِ كُلُّ شَيْءٍ لِي - أَنْتِ رُوحِي
You are my soul - You are everything to me.
Duʿā’

اللّهُمَّ ارزُقني حُبَّ من يُحبُّني فيك، واجعل بيننا مودَّةً ورحمةً، وبارك لي في زوجتي وبارك لها فيَّ، واجمعنا على طاعتك أبدًا.

“O Allah, grant me the love of the one who loves me for Your sake, place affection and mercy between us, bless me in my wife and bless her in me, and unite us always upon Your obedience.”

From your Zawjii

My dearest Chuttaa, every moment with you is paradise on earth. I pray Allah to always keeps our hearts united in love and mercy, and that we are companions in Jannah. You are my heart’s joy, answers to my duʿā, and I love you endlessly. Love You Zawjaa
